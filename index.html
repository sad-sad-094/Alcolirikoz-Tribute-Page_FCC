Here is a short biography about Alcolirykoz

Alcolirykoz is a Hip-Hop band formed in Medellín, Colombia on 2005. The band is conformed by cousins and rappers Gambeta and Kaztro with their neighbor and Dj Fa-zeta. Alcolirykoz's Compositions are highly original and influenced by other genres like salsa, bolero, jazz and soul.


This is Alkolirykoz's discography:

  • En Letras Mayúsculas (Demo) - 2007
  • La Revancha de los Tímidos - 2010
  • El Despilfarro (EP) - 2011
  • Viejas Recetas, Remixes y Otras Rarezas - 2012
  • Efectos Secundarios - 2014
  • Servicios Ambulatorioz - 2017
  • En Letras Mayúsculas (Reedición) - 2018
  • Aranjuez - 2021
